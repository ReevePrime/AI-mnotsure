SECTION 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay is a UK-based payroll and HR software and services company, widely
regarded as a market leader in its field. It was founded in 1966, giving it
over half a century of experience in the payroll industry. The company serves
businesses with up to 1,000 employees, positioning itself primarily in the
small and medium-sized business (SMB) segment of the market.

Moorepay is part of the Zellis Group, which also includes Zellis (focused on
medium-to-enterprise organisations), Benifex, and Hastee. Apax Partners LLP,
a leading global private equity advisory firm, acquired the Zellis Group from
Bain Capital. Bain Capital had originally acquired Zellis and Moorepay in 2017.

The company's headquarters are located on Lees Street in Manchester, England.
Moorepay also opened an additional office in Farnborough, Hampshire, at The
Cody Technology Park. In a significant investment in the local economy, Moorepay
committed over £100 million to a new hub at Lowry Mill in Salford, Greater
Manchester.


SECTION 2: PRODUCTS AND SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay offers an integrated suite of payroll and HR products and services,
broadly split into software and managed/outsourced services.

PAYROLL SOFTWARE
Moorepay's payroll software is cloud-based and HMRC-approved. It automates
payroll calculations, submissions, and compliance tasks. The platform is backed
by Microsoft Azure infrastructure and monitored by BITsight for security.

MANAGED PAYROLL SERVICES
For businesses that prefer to fully outsource their payroll, Moorepay provides
managed payroll services. This covers data validation, calculations, payroll
processing, BACS payments, payslip printing and distribution, and regulatory
reporting. The company maintains a 99.9% payslip accuracy rate and can implement
a basic pay run in as little as 48 hours via its Managed Emergency Payroll
Service. Managed payroll is typically suited to businesses with between 30 and
1,000 employees.

PAYROLL BUREAU SERVICES
For businesses that want expert support without full outsourcing, Moorepay
offers bureau payroll services. Expert staff handle the payroll process while
the client retains a degree of control.

HR SOFTWARE
Moorepay's HR software includes modules for:
  - Time & Attendance
  - Recruitment
  - Performance Management (including 360° feedback and social recognition)
  - Expenses Management
  - People Analytics (real-time dashboards and board-ready reporting)
  - Employee Self-Service

The HR and payroll products are offered via a single, integrated interface,
meaning payroll and HR data does not need to be manually transferred between
separate systems.

HR AND EMPLOYMENT LAW SERVICES
Moorepay also provides outsourced HR advisory services and employment law
support. These services cover topics such as compliance guidance, HR policy
advice, and health and safety.

EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
Moorepay offers an employee benefits module, supporting flexible benefits
packages that employees can personalise to their individual circumstances.

PRICING
Packages are available starting from £36 per year, with pricing scaling
according to the number of employees and the level of service selected.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Moorepay provides UK-based customer support via phone and email, available
24/7, 365 days a year. Support is also embedded natively within the software.
Support staff are CIPP (Chartered Institute of Payroll Professionals), CIPD
(Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development), and NEBOSH qualified.
Moorepay explicitly does not use ticketing systems for support queries.


SECTION 3: SCALE AND CUSTOMERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay supports a customer base of approximately 10,000 clients across the
United Kingdom. The company produces millions of payslips every year and handles
around 250,000 queries from clients annually. The team comprises around 500
dedicated payroll and HR professionals, who collectively hold over 7,000 years
of industry experience.

Notable clients include McDonald's, DHL, and Mountain Warehouse, demonstrating
Moorepay's appeal across a variety of sectors and business sizes. Moorepay
holds an "Excellent" rating on Trustpilot.


SECTION 4: ACCREDITATIONS AND AWARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay holds several significant industry accreditations and has received
notable recognition:

  - CIPP Payroll Assurance Scheme (PAS): Moorepay was the first payroll company
    to be awarded the CIPP's Payroll Assurance Scheme accreditation.
  - HMRC Approval: Moorepay is an HMRC-approved payroll software provider.
  - ISO 27001: Moorepay holds ISO 27001 certification, the international
    standard for information security management, ensuring that personal data
    is protected to the highest standards.
  - BAB Accreditation: Moorepay is also BAB (Bacs Approved Bureau) accredited.
  - Payroll Service Provider of the Year 2023: At the CIPP Annual Excellence
    Awards in October 2023, Moorepay won this award, recognising the quality
    of its payroll teams and processes.
  - EcoVadis Silver Medal: Moorepay has retained Silver Medal status in the
    EcoVadis business sustainability ratings for two consecutive years, with a
    score of 74/100, placing the company in the top 15% of assessed businesses.
  - Microhive Gold Award: Awarded in recognition of employee participation in
    the Microhive programme, which supports charitable causes.

SECTION 6: CORPORATE STRUCTURE AND OWNERSHIP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay operates as a distinct business within the Zellis Group. The Zellis
Group is made up of four businesses: Zellis, Moorepay, Benifex, and Hastee.
Each operates with autonomy to design and deliver products for their specific
customer segments. Zellis targets medium-to-enterprise organisations, while
Moorepay focuses on small and growing businesses with up to 1,000 employees.
The group's overall purpose is described as "powering exceptional employee
experiences."

The Zellis Group is currently owned by Apax Partners LLP following its
acquisition from Bain Capital. The group employs over 2,000 people across
offices in the UK, Ireland, and India.


SECTION 7: CULTURE, VALUES, AND WORKING ENVIRONMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay describes itself as a people-first organisation. Employee testimonials
highlight a professional yet relaxed working environment, with staff reporting
feeling trusted to perform their roles independently and supported in exceeding
their potential.

The group's core values, developed with input from colleagues across the
business, are:
  - We work together to achieve amazing results.
  - We are inclusive, celebrating differences and respecting others.
  - We build expertise in ourselves and others.
  - We are dynamic, always looking for better ways.
  - We work with precision and pace.
  - We take ownership for what we do, and how we do it matters.
  - We build for the future, creating repeatable, simple, and scalable solutions.

The Zellis Group is an accredited Real Living Wage employer. It is committed
to diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) and has invested in diversity training
across all operations. The group supports employee wellbeing through physical,
mental, financial, and social wellbeing programmes, including access to qualified
mental health first aiders and a clinician-designed wellbeing solution. Learning
and development programmes range from apprenticeships through to a Future
Leaders programme.

Moorepay also offers employees a flexible benefits programme (MyBenefits),
allowing individuals to customise their package, including options such as cycle-
to-work, insurance, and travel perks. Community engagement is encouraged through
volunteering days and participation in charitable programmes.


SECTION 8: ENVIRONMENTAL, SOCIAL, AND GOVERNANCE (ESG)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moorepay and the broader Zellis Group are committed to ESG responsibilities
across three key areas: wellbeing, diversity and inclusion, and environmental
impact. The group targets being operationally net carbon zero and encourages
sustainable employee behaviours through benefits such as tree planting and
greener travel options. It has retained a Silver Medal in the EcoVadis
sustainability ratings, placing it in the top 15% of businesses assessed.
